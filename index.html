How consignment works

You submit the part. Foot Me Down reviews it for safety and clinical viability, lists it with accurate details, and matches it to a verified buyer. You receive payment minus a small platform fee that sustains the program. Track the status of all your submissions under My Consignments.

Our mission, values, and impact

The problem we are solving

Children grow fast, and prosthetic components often become too small within months. The cost of new components can run into the thousands, placing an enormous burden on families navigating complex care. At the same time, clinics and prosthetists regularly see quality parts decommissioned simply because they no longer fit a patient.

Foot Me Down creates a trusted pipeline between those with parts to spare and those who need them. Every listing goes through a certified review process so buyers can trust what they receive, and sellers can feel confident their part will find the right home.

Our values

Safety first

Every component is reviewed by a certified prosthetist before listing. No exceptions.

Radical accessibility

We exist to make pediatric prosthetic care more reachable, not to profit from scarcity.

Clinician trust

We work alongside prosthetists and clinics, not around them. Verified providers are the backbone of the network.

Circular care

A part that outlives one child can transform another life. We close that loop intentionally.

Note: Foot Me Down is a prototype marketplace developed to demonstrate the concept of certified prosthetic reuse. All statistics are illustrative. We welcome feedback from clinicians, families, and prosthetists.
